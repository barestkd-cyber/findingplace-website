Why The Finding Place

Small by design. Intentional in everything.

With just a small group of children each year, every child at The Finding Place is deeply known. As a small, child-led, play-based school, our days blend discovery, hands-on materials, and generous time outdoors, because young children learn best when they are free to explore.

🌱

Nature Every Day

Outdoor play and exploration are woven into every single day — rain, shine, mud and all. Nature is our second classroom.

🧩

Reggio-Inspired

Hands-on materials, child-sized independence, and respect for each child's pace, drawn from the Reggio Emilia approach.

🏡

A Home, Not an Institution

A micro school means low ratios, calm rhythms, and real relationships — with children and with families.

Our Philosophy

Childhood isn't a race.

We believe the early years are for wondering, wandering, building, digging, asking, and becoming. As a small Christian, child-led school, we let academic readiness grow naturally out of rich play, real conversation, and a child who feels safe, known, and loved.

Our approach draws from the Reggio Emilia approach and nature-based education: child-led discovery, beautiful materials, and the East Texas outdoors as a daily teacher.
