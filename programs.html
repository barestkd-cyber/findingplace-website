A Day at The Finding Place

Calm rhythms, generous outdoor time, and real work for little hands.

Who We Serve

The Finding Place serves children roughly ages [X–X] in a mixed-age micro preschool setting. We operate [days of week], [hours]. Because we keep enrollment intentionally small, spots are limited each year — we encourage families to join our interest list early.

Our Daily Rhythm

Young children thrive on rhythm more than rigid schedules. Our days follow a predictable flow that still leaves room to chase the morning's wonder, whatever it turns out to be.

🌅

Arrival & Morning Work

A calm start. Children greet the day, settle in, and choose hands-on work from the shelves.

🍎

Snack & Practical Life

Children help prepare, serve, and clean up. Pouring, slicing (safely!), wiping tables — real skills.

🌳

Outdoor Exploration

The heart of our day. Garden work, free play, nature study, mud, bugs, and big movement.

📖

Story, Song & Circle

Language-rich time together — books, songs, rhymes, and conversation.

🎨

Open-Ended Creating

Art, building, and imaginative play where the process matters more than the product.

😴

Rest & Quiet

A peaceful reset. Little bodies and minds need stillness to grow.

What Your Child Will Gain

  • Independence: dressing themselves, managing belongings, completing real tasks start to finish.
  • Kindergarten readiness: letters, numbers, fine motor strength, and listening skills — grown naturally through play and hands-on work.
  • Social confidence: sharing, turn-taking, resolving conflicts with words, and caring for younger friends.
  • A relationship with nature: comfort, curiosity, and confidence outdoors in every season.
  • Love of learning: the one that matters most. Children who leave us still asking "why?" with sparkling eyes.
